Welcome to the tropical island of Taniti. With its sandy and rocky beaches, small, but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforesets, and a mountainours interior (with volcano) you'll sure to find something you'll enjoy.

Taniti has an area of less than 500 square mile and has an indigenous population of about 20,000. With an economny supported by fishing and agriculture, the island is growing as tourism becomes a bigger factor of the economny.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What power outlets are used on the island

    Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as the United States)

  • Are there any restrictions on alcohol?

    The drinking age in Taniti is 18.

  • Do people on Taniti speak English?

    In rual areas, espically with older residents, English is not commonly spoken. However, many youger Tanitians speak fluent English.

  • Is there a hospital on the island?

    There is one hospital and several clinic. There are many multilingual employees in the hospital.

  • Are there holidays on the island or other possible scheduling issues?

    There are many national holidays. May tourist attractions might be closed. We advise you to check the specific attraction's operating dates and times before making plans.

  • What currency is used on the island?

    Taniti uses the U.S. dollar; however, many businesses will accept euros and yen. You can exchange currency at some banks and many businesses accept major credit cards.
