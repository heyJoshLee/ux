Taniti has a range of activities. Most activities are located in Merriton Landing, a developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. Some activities include:

  • Beaches
  • Rainforest
  • An active volcano
  • History museum
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest
  • Several pubs (including a microbrewery)
  • Dance club
  • Movie theatre
  • Helicopter rides
  • Arcade
  • Art galleries
  • Bowling
  • Nine-hole golf course (opening next year)
