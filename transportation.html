Transportation while on the island includes:

  • Public buses: Taniti City, daily 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Private buses: Serving the rest of the island (please check with the specific company for more information)
  • Taxis: Taniti City
  • Rental cars: The rental agency is near the airport
  • Bikes: Various vendors offer rentals of bikes and helmets. Helments are required by law.
  • Walking: Taniti City is very walkable - especially Merriton Landing
